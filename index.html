About us

Despite the aim to move to paperless offices, businesses are producing more paper than ever. Efficient financial record-keeping and organizing systems that fit with their busy lives means hiring an extensive financial team to manually organize reams and reams of paper.

TITLE

Digitizing receipts is nothing new, but Bee’s takes it even one step further with OCR technology that reads your scanned document and turns the image into raw data that can then be categorized and organized in a spreadsheet.

TITLE

Recognizing fields such as date, HST, total amount, and the type of purchase, Bee’s Bookkeeping Engine automatically populates a spreadsheet and stores an image of the scanned receipt -- directly in your spreadsheet.

Title

When mis-filed documents can cost $125 each, and up to $700 for lost files, organized bookkeeping practices are key to your financial success.

TITLE

Your digitized receipts also ensure that your files are organized with an accurate audit trail. By assigning a visual value to every scanned document, you can easily search, organize, and retrieve documents. These paginated PDF files act as your original document, eliminating storage requirements for your hard copy documents and making reference a breeze.
